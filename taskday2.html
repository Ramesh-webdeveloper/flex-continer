Regarded as one of the most innovative and destructive, batsmen in the modern era, de Villiers is known for a range of unorthodox shots, particularly behind the wicket-keeper.[5] He made his international debut in a Test match against England in 2004 first played a One Day International (ODI) in early 2005. His debut in Twenty20 International cricket came in 2006.
